Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the brand strategy development?

Our brand strategy development includes a comprehensive analysis of your business, target audience, and competitors. We create a strategic roadmap that defines your brand positioning, messaging, visual identity, and communication guidelines. This serves as the foundation for all your marketing and design efforts.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

The timeline for website projects varies depending on the complexity and scope. Typically, our Essential package websites take 4-6 weeks, Professional package websites take 8-10 weeks, and Enterprise solutions can take 12-16 weeks or more. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your digital assets remain secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our support packages include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs.

Can I upgrade my package as my business grows?

We've designed our packages to be scalable as your business grows. You can easily upgrade to a higher-tier package or add specific services to your existing package. We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition and that your digital strategy evolves alongside your business.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For our monthly and annual packages, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment schedule that works for your business.

Do you offer any guarantees on your services?

We stand behind the quality of our work and are committed to your satisfaction. All our projects include a 30-day support period after launch to address any issues or make minor adjustments. While we can't guarantee specific business results (as these depend on many factors), we do guarantee professional, high-quality deliverables that meet the agreed-upon specifications.
